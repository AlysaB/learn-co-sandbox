Mary Jackson an inspiration aerospace engineer from the 70's.

Mary Winston Jackson had a love for science and improving the lives of people all over the world. She had her own path to an engineering career at the Nasa Langley Research Center but it was going to be hard to get. but in the end she graduated Hampton Institute with a dual degree in math and science.

Mary Jackson was the first female engineer for Nasa, so you could say she changed history. She significantly contributed to Nasa's Project Mercury
